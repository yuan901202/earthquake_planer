PACK FEATURES

- The Backpack is made of high quality shower proof 600d polyester, with PVC backing and a reinforced base section.
- The pack has extra capacity and features tuck-away padded adjustable shoulder straps in a zip pouch.
- There is reflective tape across the front, D-Rings on the corners, a carry handle on top and a zip front pouch.
- A Clear ID pocket large enough to hold a business card.
- On one side there is a webbing pouch for one of the drink bottles, while on the other side there is a shower proof clear information pouch containing a Civil Defence Check List brochure.
