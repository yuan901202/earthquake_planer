Always remeber during an earthquake - Drop, Cover & Hold!
Safe places in an earthquake


-> If you are inside,
       find a safest place, then drop, cover and hold until evacuate sign on.

-> If you are outside,
  -> Outdoors,
              stay aways buildings, trees, street lights and power lines, then drop, cover and hold.
   -> Beach or Coast,
              drop, cover and hold then move to higher ground immediately in case a tsunami follows the quake.
   -> Driving,
              pull over to a clear location, stop and stay there with your seatbelt fastened until the shaking stops. Once the shaking stops, proceed with caution and avoid bridges or ramps that might have been damaged.
   -> Mountainous Area,
              be alert for falling debris or landslides.
